Changes to Available Resources

  • 02 September 2016

    • Unit Converters are available at the bottom of the menu.
    • The menu can be hidden and shown by clicking the link near the top.
    • Statewide ASOS/AWOS Obs link has been removed...the service has been discontinued.

  • 01 July 2016

    • Added the WPC's interactive surface analysis to the Analyses section of the Observations and Analyses tab.
    • Added xmACIS2 climate and station info to the Station Information tab.
    • Added the WxBell HRRR maps to the Numerical Weather Prediction tab.

  • 25 June 2016

    • The menu can now be hidden by clicking the "Click to Hide Menu" button.
    • The menu can be displayed again by selecting the "Show Menu" button that appears in the top-left corner.
    • Unit converters are now available in the footer and have the ability to be hidden and shown.
