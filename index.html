Welcome to the personal website of Gregory Perrotte, which includes information about myself and a wealth of links to both government and university weather products. Under the WxChallenge tab, you will find an array of sorted links to various weather products arranged in an easy-to-use interface for every WxChallenge location [some are still under construction] in the 2015-16 season. A partial 2016-17 schedule has been released and the landing page has been created. The forecasting menus themselves will be created once the schedule is finalized. Under the Forecasting Menus tab, you will find similar resource menus for locations across the Carolinas [only North Carolina is currently available]. Please note that this site is not optimized for mobile devices. Sorry for the inconvenience!

Below you will find a comprehensive listing of my favorite weather products and data sources. All resources are split up by category and include a thumbnail of the website and a short description of the products itself. Please note that this collection of resources is compiled by myself and is not provided or endorsed by any of the content creators. All content is owned by their respective owners.
