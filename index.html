Welcome to my personal website which includes information about me and a wealth of links to both government and university weather products. Under the WxChallenge tab, you will find an array of sorted links to various weather products arranged in an easy-to-use interface [still under construction] for every WxChallenge location in the 2015-16 season. Pages will be built for every location in the 2016-17 season once the schedule is announced. Under the Forecasting Menus tab, you will find similar resource menus for locations across the Carolinas [also under construction].

Below you will find 2-day forecasts for Asheville, N.C. (KAVL) and Hickory, N.C. (KHKY). I will be verifying my forecasts and it is planned that these verifications will be available in the future. Two day forecasts will be issued every two days (e.g., a Monday, Tuesday will be issued on Sunday followed by a Wednesday, Thursday forecast that will be issued on Tuesday). I will do my best to issue these forecasts continually. NOTE: These forecasts have been temporarily suspended while the website is being constructed.

Please note that forecasts are currently unavailable while the interface through which forecasts are displayed is being upgraded.
