Welcome to my personal website which includes information about me and a wealth of links to both government and university weather products. Under the WxChallenge tab, you will find an array of sorted links to various weather products arranged in an easy-to-use interface [still under construction] for every WxChallenge location in the 2015-16 season. Pages will be built for every location in the 2016-17 season once the schedule is announced. Under the Carolinas Forecasting Menus tab, you will find similar resource menus for locations across the Carolinas [also under construction].

Below you will find 2-day forecasts for Asheville, N.C. (KAVL), Charlotte, N.C. (KCLT), and Hickory, N.C. (KHKY). These forecasts will be created and verified by me and it is planned that these verifications will be available in the future. I will issue two day forecasts every two days (e.g., a Monday, Tuesday will be issued on Sunday followed by a Wednesday, Thursday forecast that will be issued on Tuesday). I will do my best to issue these forecasts continually.
