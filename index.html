Welcome to my personal website which includes information about me and a wealth of links to both government and university weather products. Under the WxChallenge tab, you will find an array of sorted links to various weather products arranged in an easy-to-use interface [still under construction] for every WxChallenge location in the 2015-16 season. Pages will be built for every location in the 2016-17 season once the schedule is announced. Under the Forecasting Menus tab, you will find similar resource menus for locations across the Carolinas [only North Carolina is currently available].

Below you will find a 5-day forecast for Asheville, N.C. (KAVL). I will be verifying my forecasts and it is planned that these verifications will be available in the future. Forecasts will be issued every five days to test the accuracy of my short and medium range forecasting. I will do my best to issue these forecasts continually.

Please note that forecasts are currently unavailable while the interface through which forecasts are displayed is being upgraded.
